Meeting New Friends in the Dorm

Making connections and building friendships in my new dorm environment.


First Impressions

  • Everyone seemed so friendly during orientation. I'm excited to get to know them better.
  • My roommate, Abel, is really cool. We already bonded over our shared love of movies.

Dorm Life

  • The dorm is much busier than I expected, which is both exciting and a little overwhelming.
  • I've joined a few clubs and groups to help me meet new people.

Building Connections

  • I've started small, striking up conversations in the common areas and during activities.
  • I've found that having some shared interests really helps to build friendships.

Challenges

  • It's definitely a little intimidating to put myself out there and meet new people.
  • Balancing my social life with my studies is proving to be a bit of a challenge.

Overall

  • It's been a great experience. I'm excited about making new friends in the dorm.
  • I am feeling optimistic about my social life and friendships this semester.
