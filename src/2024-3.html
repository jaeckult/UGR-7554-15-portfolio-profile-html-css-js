A day at home after a long semester


After months of structured schedules and deadlines, a day at home feels like a breath of fresh air. Waking up without an alarm sets the tone—slow and leisurely. I often start the day sipping coffee, enjoying the quiet hum of home. The best part? Doing random things that bring joy. I’ll rummage through books I meant to read, watch comfort shows, or try cooking something experimental. Sometimes, I’ll just sit by the window, lost in thought. A day like this is about unwinding, reconnecting, and simply being myself.
